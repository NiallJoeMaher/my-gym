SWIMMING POOL & SPA



Our 18 metre swimming pool is kept at a constant, comfortable temperature and features dedicated swim lanes for your convenience. Whether your swimming to stay in shape or training for your next competition, our pool is the perfect place to get your laps in.


After your swim, why not indulge in our other facilities? Relax and rejuvenate in our jacuzzi, sauna, steam room or spa pool.


Please Note:Lane swimming must be pre-booked at all times. Guests must use a swimming hat when using the facilities. All guests using the sauna, steam room or jacuzzi must be over 16 years.
